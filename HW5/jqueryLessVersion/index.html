YOUR GAME BACKLOG

Keep track of all the games you got from across different platforms, all on one site!


IDEA BEHIND PROJECT:

Over the years, video games have become easier to obtain both monetarily and conveniently with the ease of downloadable content and various online storefronts providing sales for hundreds of popular games everyday. Because of this, it seems that a lot of gamers both casual and avid will amass large collections of digital-only video games that either simply never get played due to forgetfullness or impulsiveness to spend money.

This program was created to directly respond to said phenomenom, keep track of your collection of video games on various platforms and figure out what's been played given a proper chance as well as reconsider coming back to games once forgottren from the past. Ideally a user's list of games can be exported to be shared with others through a .txt file.
